About Me

Hi! I'm a first-year PhD student at the department of Germanic Studies at the University of Texas at Austin.

I have an MA in Computational Psycholinguistics from the University of Antwerp (Belgium), where I worked under the supervision of prof. Sarah Bernolet and prof. Orphée Declercq from Ghent University. Before that, I studied German, English and Spanish Linguistics and Literature at Ghent University.

I'm especially interested in Second Language Acquisition, Language Pedagogy and their application in the development of Computer-Assisted Language Learning tools for German, as well as the psycholinguistic study of SLA and its analysis with the use of computational tools.
