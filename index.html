Hi! I'm a first-year PhD student at the department of Germanic Studies at the University of Texas at Austin.

I have an MA in Computational Psycholinguistics from the University of Antwerp (Belgium) and I previously studied German, English and Spanish Linguistics and Literature at Ghent University.

I'm especially interested in Second Language Acquisition, Language Pedagogy and their application in the development of Computer-Assisted Language Learning tools for German.
